QuikThru

In the summer of 2018 I took a course on Design Thinking, where we learned about and practiced the three main steps of design:

  • Inspiration
  • Ideation
  • Implementation
The final assignment for the course involved making a prototype of a solution to some problem you noticed in the world.
I made a website revolving around fixing how digital boards are currently being used. I got anonymous feedback regarding my prototype and made a report about those results.

FreeDOM

Back in 2016, I went to McHacks with a couple of friends and we made a Google Chrome extension called FreeDOM.
The extension edits the appearance (or disappearance!) of any element within a webpage.
This could be useful to hide ads, enlarge/highlight the font of a section of the page before printing, etc.
